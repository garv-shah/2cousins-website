About Us

Education across borders

2cousins is a student-run non-profit that aims to bridge the gap of education between borders, linking communities from across the globe while allowing students to learn from one another. Currently, this is afforded through a vibrant tutoring program, allowing volunteers to teach classes with students from underprivileged organisations.

FAQ

2cousins is a program that aims to link communities to allow them to learn from one another, namely connecting organisations and volunteers who would like students to teach students about particular subjects.

Here’s the basic workflow:

Organisations Create Jobs

Organisations can create job listings for specific subjects that they would like to teach, defining the availability for that job (when they are free) and how often they’d like the class to repeat.

Volunteers Claim Jobs

These jobs now enter the system for any volunteer to claim given they meet the subject requirements, and volunteers fill out their availability and can sign up for classes that suit them.

Students Learn Together!

These jobs then appear on both the organisation and volunteer’s calendar, both in app and on Google Calendar. Classes can either be run in person (organised over email) or online from anywhere in the world, run on Google Meets.

Volunteer hours are tracked based on the amount of lessons you teach. You must click the "Start Shift" button for hours to be tracked, which will automatically allow you to join the lesson.

As a volunteer, we highly encourage you to spend time creating content which you can publish through our quiz/post system. As such, volunteers receive 2 volunteer hours for every hour taught in class. Please note that in America, this has been increased to 4 hours due to the different culture surrounding volunteering.

Email! Within your class page (either accessed through the schedule or manage jobs view), there will be an option to email your organisation. They will be able to rearrange class schedules for you to stay flexible with your busy schedule :)
